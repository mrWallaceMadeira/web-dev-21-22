Interview Page

Interview a classmate about their summer! Make a webpage! 

Basic requirements:
  (1) 2 HTML pages
  (2) 1 external CSS file

High marks will be awarded for webpages that are both creative and well organized. The
viewer should be able to quickly understand what ____INSERT STUDENT____ did over the 
summer. Do not be afraid to use other websites for inspiration; the console is your
friend.
